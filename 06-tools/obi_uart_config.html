What this is. A crafter for the OBI bridge's plaintext UART config channel (vsocket pipe 2). Requests are C5 5C · LEN(16-bit BE) · CRC · FE · CMD · payload; responses come back the same way with CMD | 0x80. The CRC (CRC-16/MODBUS) covers everything from FE on and may be zeroed to bypass. Reachable only over UART0 (8N1) — not BLE — and command 49 returns the 16-byte TEA key in the clear. Use only on hardware you own or are authorized to test.
  • The monitor reassembles frames out of the mixed stream; ESP-IDF console text shows as dim log lines (toggle off to hide, or on raw to also see raw hex chunks).
  • No response? Send the whole frame in one burst (this tool does) and make sure nothing else holds the port.
